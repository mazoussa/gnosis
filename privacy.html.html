Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At Gnosis Assets ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or submit an inquiry regarding our digital assets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information strictly for the purpose of facilitating business transactions and communication regarding domain asset acquisition. This includes:

  • Personal Identification: Name and email address provided via our inquiry form.
  • Transaction Details: Information regarding the specific assets you are interested in and any offer details you provide in your message.
  • Technical Data: Minimal technical logs (IP address, browser type) automatically collected by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and performance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries regarding asset availability and pricing.
  • To negotiate and facilitate the transfer of domain ownership.
  • To draft Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) if requested.
  • To comply with legal obligations related to digital asset transfers.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to complete a transaction:

  • Hosting & Forms: We use Netlify to host this website and process form submissions. Your data is processed securely through their infrastructure.
  • Escrow Services: If a sale is agreed upon, your contact information may be shared with recognized escrow services (such as Escrow.com or GoDaddy) to initiate the secure transfer of funds and assets.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Data Retention

We retain your inquiry information only as long as necessary to resolve your request or complete a transaction. If no transaction occurs, your data is periodically reviewed and deleted from our records, unless retention is required by law.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • The right to withdraw consent for communication at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption (TLS/SSL), to protect your data during transmission. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
